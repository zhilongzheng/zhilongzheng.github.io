About Me

I am a fourth-year Ph.D. student at the Department of Computer Science and Technology in Tsinghua University, advised by Prof. Jianping Wu, and co-advised by Prof. Jun Bi. I am interested in system issues of Network Function Virtualization (NFV) and Packet Processing Acceleration, focusing on their architecture, performance, management and applications. I received my B.Eng. in Software Engineering in Chongqing University in 2015.
